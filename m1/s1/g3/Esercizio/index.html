Focaccia with Caramelised Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelised onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favurite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN

Fresh Cranberry recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pairing knife, though it's not necesary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together the dough ingredients unti the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actual knead the dough, though I sometime knead it few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
